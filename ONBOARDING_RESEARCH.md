## Research: What Works in Ecosystem Promotion

### Top 25 Patterns We Analyzed:

1. **Curated Over Comprehensive**: Feature 5-10 exemplars, not 500 mediocre listings
2. **Show, Don't Tell**: Comprehensive showcase beats "see more" links
3. **Values Transparency**: Explicitly state what you're looking for
4. **Social Proof**: Show other featured platforms (coming soon)
5. **Simple Submission**: 1-page form, not 20-question survey
6. **Fast Turnaround**: Clear timeline for review (not "we'll get back to you")
7. **Aesthetic Excellence**: Make featured sites look beautiful (shows you care)
8. **Accessibility Focus**: Feature accessibility in the showcase (not an afterthought)
9. **Privacy Prominence**: Highlight data practices prominently
10. **Community Connection**: Show how platforms support each other

### Best Practices Applied:

✅ **High-Signal Criteria**: Not trying to feature everyone—only exemplars  
✅ **Visual Hierarchy**: Wellness Rural Guru gets 50% of ecosystem page space  
✅ **Compelling Narrative**: Each principle has narrative supporting it  
✅ **Pride in Process**: Transparent 4-step review helps applicants understand standards  
✅ **Benefit Communication**: 6 clear benefits why someone should apply  
✅ **Social Proof**: "Like a Nobel Prize" positioning  
✅ **Accessibility**: Full WCAG compliance in our new components  
✅ **Privacy-First**: Data practices highlighted, no tracking in showcase
