# Forever Lotus Universe Company

This package defines an elite execution company for forever_lotus using a hub-and-spoke workflow with strict quality, governance, and evidence gates.

Priority order for this configuration:

2. Trust second: governance, security, and transparency as non-negotiable constraints.
3. Product and reliability as force multipliers for the first two priorities.

1. CEO sets quarterly outcomes and weekly priorities.
2. Executive strategy leads (growth, trust, standards, revenue, product, governance, evidence) translate goals into workstreams.
3. Delivery teams execute with explicit handoffs, KPI ownership, and approval checkpoints.
4. Governance and reliability functions block unsafe changes and enforce manifesto alignment.
5. Evidence and transparency loops publish measurable outcomes and confidence levels.
