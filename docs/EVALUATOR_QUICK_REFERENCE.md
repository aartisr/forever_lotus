# Manifesto Evaluator - Quick Reference

**For:** Organizations & developers using the Forever Lotus Manifesto Evaluation Engine  
**Version:** 1.0.0  

---

## 🚀 Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Access the Evaluator

1. Visit `/evaluate` on your Forever Lotus installation
2. Enter your website URL: `https://your-website.org`
3. Click "Start Evaluation"
4. View your results on the beautiful dashboard

### What You'll Get

✓ **Overall Compliance Score** (0-100)  
✓ **Letter Grade** (A+ to D)  
✓ **Seven Principle Scores** - How you align with each manifesto principle  
✓ **Page-by-Page Results** - Detailed assessments of key pages  
✓ **Strengths** - What you're doing exceptionally well  
✓ **Opportunities** - Areas for growth  
✓ **Critical Next Steps** - Prioritized actions to improve  
✓ **Checklist Mapping** - See if you meet onboarding criteria

## 🎯 22 Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Compassion & Non-Domination (3 criteria)

- [ ] **Non-Extractive Language** - No manipulative, fear-based messaging
- [ ] **Accessibility First** - WCAG compliance, alt text, inclusive design
- [ ] **Inclusive Design** - Marginalized voices welcomed and centered

### 2. Kindness Without Expectation (3 criteria)

- [ ] **Authentic Purpose** - Clear mission, not jargon-filled
- [ ] **No Dark Patterns** - Honest UX, no addiction tactics
- [ ] **One-Way Value** - You give value; don't extract data/attention

### 3. Earth Consciousness (3 criteria)

- [ ] **Environmental Statement** - You acknowledge ecological responsibility
- [ ] **Sustainable Practices** - Carbon-aware hosting, minimal tech bloat
- [ ] **Supply Chain Transparency** - Clear about sourcing and ethics

### 4. Humanitarian Dignity (3 criteria)

- [ ] **Empowerment** - Content empowers, doesn't create dependency
- [ ] **Respect & Autonomy** - Users have choice and clear opt-outs
- [ ] **Voice & Representation** - Beneficiaries participate in decisions

### 5. Education & Liberation (3 criteria)

- [ ] **Knowledge Liberation** - Learning expands agency and critical thinking
- [ ] **Accessible Learning** - Free/low-cost, multilingual where possible
- [ ] **Skill & Agency Growth** - You produce skills, not just information

### 6. Peace & Inner Harmony (3 criteria)

- [ ] **Conflict-Aware Communication** - Nuanced, reduces polarization
- [ ] **Transparency & Trust** - Clear data, decision-making practices
- [ ] **Inner Practice** - Calm, clear, unhurried tone throughout

### 7. Transparency & Agency (3 criteria)

- [ ] **Clear Ownership** - Leadership and purpose are obvious
- [ ] **Privacy & Data** - Clear policy, minimal collection, no deception
- [ ] **Impact Metrics** - You share real impact, not vanity metrics

## 🔧 Common Improvements

### Raise Compassion Score
- Add alt text to images
- Test site with accessibility tools (Color Contrast, Screen Reader)
- Replace fear-based language with inspiring language
- Make donation/sign-up optional, not mandatory

### Raise Kindness Score
- Rewrite homepage to clearly state "Who you are and why you exist" in plain English
- Remove "confirm email" popups on first visit
- Stop emailing people without explicit consent
- Show unsubscribe link at bottom of every email

### Raise Earth Consciousness
- Add 1 sentence about environmental responsibility to your site
- Review hosting provider - use green hosting if possible
- Optimize images and reduce site bloat
- Link to your sustainability practices or commitments

### Raise Dignity Score
- Add "About" page with real team photos and stories
- Include beneficiary voices and testimonials
- Make it easy to opt-out of tracking
- Use simple language, avoid savior narratives

### Raise Education Score
- Feature your learning resources prominently
- Make key resources free or low-cost
- Add a blog/insights section with actionable content
- Provide worksheets, guides, or toolkits people can download

### Raise Peace Score
- Write about complexity - instead of "we have all answers," say "here's how we approach this nuanced issue"
- Add a privacy page that's actually readable
- Share data on your impact (be honest about limitations)
- Avoid superlatives ("best," "only," "revolutionary")

### Raise Transparency
- Add an "About Us" page with founder names and bios
- Link to a clear Privacy Policy
- Share annual metrics or impact reports
- Explain your business model - how do you make money?

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: My score is low. Does this mean my organization is bad?

**A:** No. The evaluator assesses alignment with the Forever Lotus Manifesto specifically. You might be doing amazing work in a different framework. This is just feedback on one dimension.

### Q: Can I appeal or get a second evaluation?

**A:** Yes! This first version is automated. Real humans will review submissions before final certification. But first, make real improvements.

### Q: How often should I re-evaluate?

**A:** Evaluate quarterly as you make changes. Show progress over time. It's a growth journey, not a one-time test.

### Q: What if our site doesn't fit a criterion perfectly?

**A:** That's okay. Criteria allow for "Partial" and "Not Applicable" ratings. Perfection isn't the goal—authentic alignment is.

### Q: Can I use this for my department/project/team?

**A:** Absolutely. The evaluator is generic. You can adapt it for internal projects, partnerships, grantees, or vendors.

### Q: How do I get certified?

**A:** (When certification launches) Complete the evaluation, score 80+, finish onboarding, and a human reviewer will approve your "Forever Lotus Certified" status.
