## **EMAIL SENDING BEST PRACTICES**

1. **Personalization:** Every email should reference specific [recent article / initiative / quote]
   - NOT generic
   - NOT templated
   - REAL evidence you understand their work

2. **Subject Line:** Should include their org name + what we're offering
   - ✓ "GiveDirectly + Awaricon: Proof of Impact"
   - ✗ "Partnership Opportunity (Too generic)
   - ✗ "Awaricon for Your Organization" (impersonal)

3. **Opening:** Start with their achievement, not our pitch
   - ✓ "I was reading GiveDirectly's transparency work..."
   - ✗ "We're building Awaricon, a new certification system..."

4. **CTA:** Always include [Demo] + [Call booking link]
   - Demo: 2-min video showing how Awaricon works
   - Call: Calendly or similar booking link (30 min slots, Tue-Fri)

5. **Timing:** Send Tuesday-Wednesday morning (10-11am their timezone)
   - Monday: Too many emails in inbox
   - Thursday-Friday: Decision fatigue, lower response

6. **Follow-up:** After 5 days (if no response), send ONE follow-up only
   - Keep it short: "Just wanted to circle back... still interested?"
   - Add new info: "3 organizations have shown interest..."

## **SUCCESS METRICS PER EMAIL**

- **Open Rate Target:** 60-80% (personalized cold email)
- **Click Rate Target:** 30-40% (to demo or call link)
- **Response Rate Target:** 20-30% (direct email reply)
- **Demo Conversion:** 50-70% of responders → demo call booked
- **Commitment Rate:** 50-80% of demo calls → interested in Platinum

**If below targets:**
- Rewrite subject lines (A/B test personalization angles)
- Simplify CTA (one button, not multiple links)
- Shorten email (3 short paragraphs, not 5)
- Add social proof (mention other orgs considering it)
