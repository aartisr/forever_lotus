# Forever Lotus Logo Rationale

This logo system is designed directly from the manifesto in [MANIFESTO.md](MANIFESTO.md).

## Core Symbolism

- Rising lotus form: represents disciplined hope, transcendence through conditions, and moral evolution.
- Rooted base arcs: represent being rooted in reality and ecosystem, not detached idealism.
- Water lines under the bloom: represent the principle "rooted, rising, untainted".
- Center seed: represents conscious creation and responsibility (creation is moral, not neutral).
- Dual petal palette:
  - gold = dignity, stewardship, continuity
  - rose = compassion, humanity, non-domination

## Manifesto Mapping

- Section I and II (Meaning and Lotus Across Civilization): expressed by the lotus-first geometry and cross-cultural symbolism.
- Section III (Conscious Creation): expressed by the central seed core.
- Section IV (Kindness Without Expectation): expressed by open, outward petals with no aggressive angles.
- Section V and XIV (Earth Consciousness and Continuity): expressed by the rooted base and water line foundation.
- Section VIII (Peace and Inner Harmony): expressed by radial symmetry and balanced center.
- Section IX (A Vow in Substance): expressed by restrained, non-extractive visual language.

## Usage Guidance

- Use `variant="nav"` for compact navigation contexts.
- Use `variant="section"` for section headers and cards.
- Use `variant="hero"` where symbolic depth and atmosphere are desired.

## Design Integrity Rules

- Do not stretch or skew the mark.
- Keep center seed visible in all variants.
- Preserve base arcs to retain the "rooted" meaning.
- Avoid replacing the palette with high-saturation neon themes that undermine the manifesto tone.
