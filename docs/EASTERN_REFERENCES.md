## C) Brahma and Conscious Creation: Primary Religious/Philosophical Texts

These are not modern university papers; they are canonical textual foundations.

### Vedic and Upanishadic sources
- Rigveda 10.129 (Nasadiya Sukta): creation hymn and metaphysical origins.
- Chandogya Upanishad 3.14.1: "sarvam khalvidam brahma" (all this is Brahman).
- Taittiriya Upanishad 3.1.1: "from which beings are born..." cosmological formulation.
- Mundaka Upanishad 1.1.7-1.1.9: emanation metaphors and source of creation.

### Puranic sources (Brahma-lotus narrative)
- Bhagavata Purana, Skandha 3 (esp. creation sequence around Brahma emerging on the lotus from Vishnu's navel).
- Vishnu Purana, Book 1 (creation narrative including Brahma's role).

Usage note: For publication-grade work, cite a specific critical edition/translator and publisher in your preferred style guide (APA/Chicago).
