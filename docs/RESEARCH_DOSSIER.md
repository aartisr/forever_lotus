## G. Authenticity Guardrails (Mandatory)

1. Do not cite social posts, anonymous summaries, or AI-generated references as primary evidence.
2. For each key claim, retain at least one DOI or university-hosted citation.
3. Keep scripture and modern empirical studies in separate sections.
4. Add access date when referencing dynamic web pages.
5. Preserve source titles exactly and avoid paraphrasing bibliographic metadata.
