# World-Class Research and Validation Standard

## Goal
Ensure Forever Lotus claims are independently verifiable and replicable.

## Validation Ladder

1. Internal evidence
- Pilot metrics with transparent methodology.

2. External evaluation
- Independent partner validates design and analysis.

3. Replication
- At least two independent contexts reproduce signal.

4. Publication
- Peer-reviewed or equivalent externally reviewed output.

## Minimum Standard for Major Claims

A claim can be called strategic impact only if all are true:
1. Baseline and post-intervention data exist.
2. Method and limitations are published.
3. Third-party review has occurred.
4. Result is durable for at least one full reporting cycle.

## Partner Model

1. University and research lab partners.
2. NGO field implementation partners.
3. Independent methodology reviewers.

## Annual Evidence Outputs

1. Impact methodology report.
2. Independent validation summary.
3. Replication status brief.
