# World-Class Transparency Standard

## Objective
Build trust through regular, auditable disclosures of performance and limitations.

## Public Reporting Pack

1. Impact snapshot.
2. KPI trend summary.
3. Methods and assumptions.
4. Risks and unresolved issues.
5. Corrective actions in progress.

## Transparency Rules

1. No major claim without evidence link.
2. Report uncertainty and limitations explicitly.
3. Version all public reports.
4. Keep historical reports accessible.

## Reporting Cadence

1. Quarterly public transparency note.
2. Annual full impact and methodology report.
