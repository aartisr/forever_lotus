## **Asset 5: Demo Call Outline/Script**

**Purpose:** When prospect books a call, we're prepared to:
1. Understand their pain
2. Show how Awaricon solves it
3. Get their buy-in on next steps

**What's Included:**

```
30-Minute Demo Call Structure:

[0-2 min] OPENING
└─ "Thanks for taking 30 minutes. Quick question first: 
   What brought you here?" (Listen: their actual pain point)

[2-5 min] UNDERSTANDING THEIR WORLD
├─ "So you're focused on [their challenge]..."
├─ "How is [pain point] impacting [their org] right now?"
└─ Listen for: Where does lack of trust show up?

[5-7 min] INTRODUCE AWARICON
├─ "This is exactly why we built Awaricon"
├─ Show: Website `/awaricon/apply`
└─ Explain: 4-tier system + scoring

[7-12 min] LIVE DEMO
├─ "Let me walk through how [Organization] would use this"
├─ Demo: Application process (2 min)
├─ Demo: How badge appears on site (2 min)
├─ Demo: Admin dashboard + analytics (1 min)
└─ Ask: "Does this feel relevant to what you're working on?"

[12-20 min] BUSINESS CASE
├─ Show: ROI for their sector
├─ Example: "Media orgs see 2-3x more premium subscribers"
├─ Ask: "How would this impact your organization?"
└─ Listen for: Interest level, objections, timeline

[20-25 min] NEXT STEPS
├─ If interested: "Would you be interested in becoming a 
    Founding Platinum member?"
├─ Explain: Benefits (case study, early access, board role)
├─ Timeline: "We're moving fast. Decision by Week 4?"
└─ Action: "I'll send you [case study / implementation guide]"

[25-30 min] CLOSING
├─ "Any final questions?"
├─ "I'll follow up within 24 hours with [specific commitment]"
└─ Thank them for their time
```
