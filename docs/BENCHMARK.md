## What to learn from this benchmark
1. Clear moral center and plain-language mission.
2. Strong editorial standards and source transparency.
3. Coherent navigation architecture (research, action, learning, impact).
4. Human-first storytelling over promotional language.
5. Consistent design language that signals trust and calm authority.
