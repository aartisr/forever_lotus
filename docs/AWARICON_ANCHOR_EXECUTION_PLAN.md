## **KEY SUCCESS FACTORS**

### ✅ Do This
1. **Personalize heavily** — Every email references their specific work
2. **Move fast** — Cold email → Demo call within 5 days
3. **Show ROI** — Tell each org how Awaricon solves THEIR pain
4. **Follow up relentlessly** — Phone call + email + LinkedIn (don't spam, but persist)
5. **Listen on demos** — Their questions reveal what matters most
6. **Case study mindset** — From day 1, plan how you'll tell their success story

### ❌ Avoid This
1. **Generic templates** — Each email should feel like it was written for THEM only
2. **Long delays** — If they respond, book within 24 hours
3. **Generic ROI** — Don't use media ROI on NGOs; customize by sector
4. **Email only** — Use email + LinkedIn + phone if needed
5. **One-touch follow-up** — If no response, follow up 1x (5 days later)
6. **Boring demos** — Live, interactive, tell a story (don't just show features)

## **COMMON OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES**

### **Objection 1: "We're not sure what an Awaricon badge means for our org."**

**Response:**
"That's exactly right to ask. Here's what it tells your stakeholders:
- You completed rigorous third-party verification
- You passed human review (not algorithm)
- You're accountable for your claims about [mission/values/transparency]

The badge on your site = 'We're verified real, and we invite scrutiny.'"

### **Objection 2: "This feels like another certification we don't need."**

**Response:**
"I get it. But here's the difference: Awaricon isn't adding compliance burden.
It's solving a customer problem: 'How do I know this organization is real?'

Your donors/customers get peace of mind. You get conversion lift. Win-win."

### **Objection 3: "What's the cost?"**

**Response:**
"No cost for founding Platinum members. We're investing in your participation 
because your credibility helps set the standard.

Later (Q3 2026), we'll offer tiered pricing for scale. But you're grandfathered 
in free forever."

### **Objection 4: "How long does implementation take?"**

**Response:**
"2-3 weeks from approval to live badge on your site. Main work:
- Add 3-line JavaScript code (or use WordPress plugin)
- Choose badge style + placement
- Test on staging environment

Our integration guide handles 90% of it. You can DIY or we provide support."

### **Objection 5: "What if we don't want our score public?"**

**Response:**
"Totally optional. You can:
- Display badge but not show score (just tier)
- Display score but not mission/values breakdown
- Display everything (recommended for transparency)

It's your choice how visible to be."

## **RED FLAGS & HOW TO HANDLE**

### 🚩 They go silent after demo call
→ Send follow-up email same day  
→ If no response in 3 days, send ONE more email with social proof  
→ If still silent after 5 days, move on (low-priority org)

### 🚩 They say "Let me discuss with my board"
→ Good sign (they're considering seriously)  
→ Ask: "When does your board meet? Can I send materials for discussion?"  
→ Offer: "Happy to join a 15-min call with your board/leadership team"

### 🚩 They ask "Are there any other orgs doing this?"
→ Great sign (they want social proof)  
→ Response: "We're selecting 5-10 founding members. Can't share names yet, but I can tell you sectors: NGO, media, tech, education, brand."  
→ Follow-up: "If you commit now, you'll be among the earliest."

### 🚩 They're interested but want to wait until [date]
→ Set a specific decision date  
→ Say: "Perfect. How about we lock in a 15-min check-in call on [date]?"  
→ Create urgency (but honestly): "We're moving quickly, so I want to make sure you're in the loop."
