# World-Class Content and Evidence Standard

## Purpose
Ensure public content is accurate, evidence-backed, and repeatable in quality.

## Content Rules

1. Claims must be evidence-linked.
2. Language should be precise, non-inflationary, and auditable.
3. Distinguish aspiration from measured result.

## Editorial Workflow

1. Draft by domain owner.
2. Evidence review by research owner.
3. Style and clarity review.
4. Publication with date/version.

## Required Metadata Per Major Document

1. Owner.
2. Last updated date.
3. Evidence sources.
4. Known limitations.

## Content Cadence

1. Weekly: tactical updates.
2. Monthly: impact progress bulletin.
3. Quarterly: evidence and strategy synthesis.
