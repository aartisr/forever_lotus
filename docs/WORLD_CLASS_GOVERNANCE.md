# World-Class Governance Standard

## Governance Objectives

1. Maintain mission integrity as scale grows.
2. Prevent conflicts of interest and ethics drift.
3. Provide transparent decision and escalation paths.

## Required Governance Structures

1. Advisory Board
- Independent members across humanitarian, research, and ethics domains.

2. Ethics Review Process
- Defined intake, triage, review, and closure workflow.

3. Conflict of Interest Register
- Mandatory disclosure and recusal protocol.

4. Policy Set
- Data governance policy.
- Responsible communication policy.
- Program integrity policy.

## Decision Rights

1. Program decisions: Program Lead + Evidence Lead.
2. Public claims: Communications Lead + Evidence Lead.
3. Ethical exceptions: Ethics Committee review required.

## Governance Cadence

1. Monthly governance review.
2. Quarterly external advisor review.
3. Annual policy revision and public summary.
